# Cheap Regeneration

A trajectory cannot escape forever using only a finite amount of 2-adic shadow.

Escape consumes shadow.

Therefore, an escaping trajectory must regenerate shadow.

A regeneration is dangerous when it produces large future escape potential at low compression cost.

This is called cheap regeneration.

The central target is:

    no infinite cheap regeneration chain in positive odd Collatz dynamics

A possible route is to show that every infinite chain of regenerations must eventually force average debt above:

    log2(3)

If so, escape becomes impossible.
