# Conservation of Instability

Version: v3.8

Instability can itself be conserved.

This means the recurring behavior is not a stable form, but the failure of certain forms to stabilize.

## Why this matters

If dangerous forms repeatedly fail to stabilize, the conserved behavior may be instability itself.

That is a native structural fact.

## Boundary

Conserved instability does not prove that obstruction-stable grammar cannot exist.

It only describes the bounded grammar observed.
