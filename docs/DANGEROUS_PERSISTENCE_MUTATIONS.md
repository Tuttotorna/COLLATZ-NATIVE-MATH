# Dangerous Persistence Mutations

Version: v3.6

Dangerous persistence occurs when dangerous grammar remains in a dangerous family across mutation.

## Meaning

This is a stress signal.

It is not obstruction by itself.

## Why it matters

Dangerous persistence shows that Collatz can keep speaking close to obstruction without necessarily preserving obstruction.

That distinction is central:

dangerous persistence is not obstruction persistence.

## Required for obstruction

A real native obstruction would require more than dangerous persistence.

It would require:

- persistent debt
- non-erased shadow
- dangerous regeneration
- insufficient compensation
- no closure event
- internal admissibility

## Boundary

v3.6 maps dangerous persistence.

It does not declare obstruction.
