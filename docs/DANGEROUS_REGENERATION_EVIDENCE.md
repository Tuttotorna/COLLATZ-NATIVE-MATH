# Dangerous Regeneration Evidence

Version: v2.7

Dangerous regeneration is a stress condition.

It is not obstruction by itself.

## Dangerous regeneration means

Dangerous regeneration means renewed debt appears after compensation while stress indicators remain.

Examples of stress indicators:

- tight positive surplus;
- long recovery distance;
- many regenerated bad windows;
- weak compensation margin;
- repeated local instability.

## What v2.6 showed

v2.6 found many dangerous-regeneration events.

That matters because the scanner did not merely test easy cases.

It found stress, but not obstruction candidate behavior.

## Boundary

Dangerous regeneration does not equal obstruction.

Obstruction requires persistent unclosed debt.

The distinction remains central.
