# Dangerous Stability

Version: v3.7

Dangerous stability means dangerous native grammar persists under mutation.

It is not obstruction by itself.

But it is more important than isolated danger.

## Required distinction

dangerous sentence != dangerous stability

dangerous stability != obstruction

obstruction requires self-preserving unclosed debt

## Why dangerous stability matters

A dangerous sentence may be temporary.

A dangerous stable sentence may be a stronger grammar signal.

The project must distinguish those two cases.

## Boundary

v3.7 detects bounded dangerous stability signals.

It does not claim infinite dangerous stability.

It does not claim proof.
