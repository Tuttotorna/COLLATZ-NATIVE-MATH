# Native-Standard Glossary

Version: v2.0

This document gives a controlled glossary.

## Native: expansion

Standard: pressure introduced by the 3n + 1 operation.

## Native: discharge

Standard: power-of-two removal after expansion.

## Native: debt

Standard: deficit created when discharge is insufficient relative to expansion pressure.

## Native: bad window

Standard: interval of odd blocks whose average discharge lies below escape pressure.

## Native: compensation

Standard: later interval whose discharge restores positive combined margin.

## Native: surplus

Standard: positive compensation margin.

## Native: shadow

Standard: persistence of deficit influence after local recovery.

## Native: regeneration

Standard: recurrence of deficit windows after compensation.

## Native: obstruction

Standard: admissible persistent deficit structure that avoids sufficient compensation.

## Native: closure

Standard: erasure or impossibility of persistent non-compensation.

## Constraint

The glossary is directional but reversible.

Native objects define the meaning.

Standard terms express the meaning.
