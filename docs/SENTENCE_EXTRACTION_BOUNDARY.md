# Sentence Extraction Boundary

Version: v3.3

This document defines what the Native Sentence Extractor is allowed to claim.

## Allowed

The extractor may say:

- a debt sentence was detected;
- a compensation sentence was detected;
- a regeneration sentence was detected;
- a dangerous regeneration sentence was detected;
- no obstruction sentence was extracted from the finite source artifact.

## Forbidden

The extractor may not say:

- Collatz is solved;
- global closure is proved;
- no obstruction can exist;
- dangerous regeneration is obstruction;
- native sentence extraction is proof;
- reaching 1 is the meaning of the sentence.

## Negative result boundary

No obstruction sentence extracted in a finite source artifact is not proof that obstruction cannot exist.

## Correct role

The extractor is a language-learning instrument.

It helps the project hear Collatz before translating Collatz.
