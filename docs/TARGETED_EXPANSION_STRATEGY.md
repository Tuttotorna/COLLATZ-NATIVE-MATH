# Targeted Expansion Strategy

Version: v2.8

The v2.8 scanner expands the search only where the native structure says stress is meaningful.

## Why not brute force

Blind brute force answers the wrong question.

The native method is not looking for large numbers.

It is looking for obstruction structure.

## Expansion sources

The scanner selects seeds from prior evidence:

- high dangerous regeneration count;
- high debt-window count;
- high recovery stress;
- tight positive surplus;
- known anchors.

Then it scans local neighborhoods around those seeds.

## Native reason

If obstruction-preserving regeneration exists near the currently visible boundary, it should appear near stress concentrations.

Therefore v2.8 expands around stress concentrations.

## Boundary

Targeted expansion is still finite.

It strengthens evidence.

It does not prove global closure.
