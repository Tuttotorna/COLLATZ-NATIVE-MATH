# v2.9 Limits

Version: v2.9

This document states the limits of v2.9.

## Limit 1: bounded source

v2.9 depends on v2.8 bounded finite evidence.

Therefore, v2.9 inherits the finite-domain boundary.

## Limit 2: instrumentation, not proof

v2.9 classifies shadow signals.

It does not prove that shadow must erase.

It does not prove that obstruction cannot exist.

## Limit 3: signal, not theorem

A persistent shadow signal is not a theorem.

It is a structural indicator.

## Limit 4: no global closure claim

Even if no obstruction-candidate shadow appears, that is only a finite negative result.

## Correct negative result

Correct:

No obstruction-preserving shadow was detected in this finite instrumentation layer.

Incorrect:

No obstruction-preserving shadow can exist.
